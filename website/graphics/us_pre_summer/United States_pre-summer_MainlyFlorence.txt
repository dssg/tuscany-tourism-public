In the heatmap above, we can see the density of the visits of all tourists belonging to the <i>Mainly Florence</i> cluster. The darker the colour, the more Mainly Florence visited that municipality during the pre-summer season (May 2017). As we can see from the  heatmap, the majority of the tourists from  this cluster visited Firenze (66.0% of the tourists in this cluster). The next most visited municipalities are Montepulciano (25.0%), and Poggibonsi (24.0%).
